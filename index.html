Infinity Stones

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are six stones below. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lost the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each stone.
